What is Blockchain-Ads?

Blockchain-Ads its a Web3 advertising platform, developing a revolutionary way of user interacting with ads on the web.

Who can Advertise?

No Censorship. It's a decentralized ad platform, where everyone on the internet can advertise their product or service.

How can use the platform?

Register and login. You will gain full access to the server capabilities.

How do I benefit from this Ad platform?

Advertisers: No censorship, Lowest CPM, Decentralized payment, Removed ad blocking.
Publishers: Lowest fees, No intermediaries, Direct interation with the advertisers, Instant payments.
Users: Have full control of thier data, Choose what ads they want to see, Get paid for it.

What Ad formats does Blockchain-Ads support?

Image ads, HTML ads and direct ads.

What targeting capabilites does the Ad platform has?

The higest level of tageting capabilities is applicable and you can target on User level, Device level, Website level. More over there is an oportunity to target Extensions Users, such as MetaMask users.

How do I pay for it?

The simplest way is to use the third party tool for payment where you can choose to pay in more then 40 different cryptocurrencies.

Is there a Reporting and Analytics tool?

You can access the reporting dashboard in the platform and check all your campaigns' performance.

Is there a token?

Blockchain-Ads does not have its own token yet. Once released Blockchain-Ads native token XADS will be integrated and will be used for transactions in the platform

Type of Token

XADS its a BSC-20 (BSC) token with its respective counterpairs in Polygon and Etherium and Solana
